About Alster-Mobile

Welcome to Alster-Mobile, where innovation meets quality. We specialize in designing and delivering container houses that serve as modern offices and cozy living spaces. Our team is dedicated to excellence and has a proven track record of transforming containers into functional environments.

Our business model is Design-Build. Our Modular Services include Design, Engineering, Permitting, Factory Construction, Site Development, and Construction Management. We also provide Consulting Services to real estate developers and investors as they navigate the complexities of factory-built housing.
